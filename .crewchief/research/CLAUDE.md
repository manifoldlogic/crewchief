# .crewchief/research - Exploratory Technical Research

Pre-project exploration and technical investigation.

## Purpose

Research that may inform future projects but isn't tied to a specific project yet. Exploratory investigations, technology evaluations, and architectural research.

## What Belongs Here

- Technology evaluations and comparisons
- Architecture exploration documents
- Research that spans multiple potential projects
- Investigation notes before project scoping

## What Does NOT Belong Here

- Project-specific analysis (use `projects/*/planning/`)
- Point-in-time reports (use `reports/`)
- Permanent documentation (use `/docs/`)
- Temporary notes (use `scratchpad/`)

## Lifecycle

When research becomes a project:
1. Create project in `projects/`
2. Synthesize relevant research into `planning/analysis.md`
3. Research doc can remain here as historical reference
