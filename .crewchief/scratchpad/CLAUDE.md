# .crewchief/scratchpad - Temporary Working Space

Ephemeral notes, experiments, and work-in-progress.

## Purpose

Temporary holding area for ideas, experiments, and notes that haven't found a permanent home. Content here may be cleaned up periodically.

## What Belongs Here

- Quick notes and ideas
- Experimental code snippets
- Draft content being developed
- Temporary files during investigation

## What Does NOT Belong Here

- Anything that should persist long-term
- Project planning (use `projects/`)
- Research worth preserving (use `research/`)
- Formal reports (use `reports/`)

## Cleanup Policy

Content older than 30 days may be reviewed for cleanup. Promote valuable content to appropriate permanent locations before it becomes stale.
